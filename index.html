BasketStats

Track basketball shot charts and game stats with a fast, local-first game tracker.

BasketStats is a local-first basketball stat tracker for recording shots, quarter-by-quarter box score details, heatmaps, and exportable PDF reports. This site is the public home for support, privacy, and legal information required for App Store distribution.

Shot chart tracking with make or miss markers, free throws, and per-quarter context
Quick game stat entry for assists, rebounds, fouls, and turnovers
Optional BasketStats Pro unlock for full heatmaps and PDF reports, with purchases handled by Apple

Privacy-forward by design: BasketStats works locally on your device, does not include third-party ads, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Who It Is For

BasketStats is built for coaches, trainers, players, parents, and teams who want a simple way to record shot location and core game statistics during or after a game.

What It Does Best

The app focuses on quick in-game stat capture, clear shot charts, and exportable game reports without forcing account setup or cloud infrastructure.
