Privacy Policy for BasketStats

Effective date: 2026-04-11

Back to main page

BasketStats ("the App") is developed and published by Dominik Schmitt ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes, how that data is used, and what choices you have.

Summary

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • App data is stored locally on your device.
  • Generated PDF reports are stored locally in the app container unless you choose to remove them.

Data We Process

BasketStats is designed to work primarily on-device. The App may process the following categories of data:

1. Data you create in the App

This may include:

  • shot chart entries, including shot location, make or miss result, shot type, quarter, and timestamp
  • game statistics such as assists, rebounds, fouls, and turnovers
  • court calibration points used to align the court overlay
  • app settings such as selected language and the currently active screen
  • the current game state needed to restore your session after backgrounding or relaunch

Purpose:

  • to provide the App's core functionality
  • to save your game and restore it when you reopen the App
  • to generate accurate shot charts and game summaries

2. Exported report files

If you export a game report, the App generates a PDF file locally on your device.

This may include:

  • shot charts
  • quarter summaries
  • assists, rebounds, fouls, and turnovers
  • totals and timestamps contained in the report

Purpose:

  • to let you review, keep, or share a report of the recorded game

The App does not upload exported reports to our own servers.

3. Support communications

If you contact us by email, we will process the information you include in that message, such as your email address, device details, app version, and problem description.

Purpose:

  • to answer support requests
  • to investigate bugs or app behavior

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect or receive through the App:

  • your name
  • phone number
  • precise location
  • contacts
  • photos
  • browsing history
  • health data
  • payment information
  • user-generated content uploaded to our servers

We also do not use the App to profile users for advertising or behavioral marketing.

Tracking

BasketStats does not track you across third-party apps, websites, or services.

The App's privacy-related configuration states that tracking is disabled and that no data is collected for tracking purposes.

Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and it does not operate a backend service for gameplay data.

The App may rely on Apple platform services and frameworks, including local file storage, document preview, and system email handling when you choose to contact support.

These Apple services are governed by Apple's own terms, policies, and platform behavior.

Apple Privacy Policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing

Where applicable under privacy laws, we process data because it is necessary:

  • to provide the functionality of the App
  • to store and restore your game state and settings
  • to generate local PDF reports when you request export
  • to respond to support requests you send to us

Data Retention

Data stored locally in the App is retained until:

  • you reset the game or otherwise replace it in the App
  • you delete exported PDF files from the app's storage context
  • you delete the App from your device
  • your device or system settings remove the stored app data

Support emails are retained for as long as reasonably necessary to handle your request and any follow-up related to that request.

Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to the nature of the App to help protect information processed by BasketStats. App data remains in the app sandbox on your device, and exported files are written with local file protection enabled by the app where supported. However, no method of electronic storage, transmission, or processing can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You can:

  • reset the current game in the App
  • change the app language in the App
  • export a PDF report or choose not to export one
  • delete the App to remove local app data from your device
  • choose whether to contact support by email

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of processing of your personal data, and where applicable, the right to object to certain processing or request data portability.

If you would like to make a privacy-related request, contact us at basketstats.support@gmail.com. We will review and respond in accordance with applicable law. Because BasketStats primarily stores information on your device, some requests may need to be fulfilled by deleting data within the App or deleting the App from your device.

Children's Privacy

BasketStats is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

International Users

If you use the App outside the country where the developer is based, information processed by the App is generally stored on your device. If you email support, that communication may be processed using standard email infrastructure in countries outside your own, depending on your email provider and ours.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

Data Breach Response

If we become aware of a personal data breach affecting information under our control, we will take steps we consider appropriate under the circumstances and will provide any notifications required by applicable law.
